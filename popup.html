Keyboard shortcuts help you move quickly:

  • Space toggle the current tab selection.
  • Ctrl+Enter (or ⌘+Enter) closes selected tabs.
  • J moves to the tab above, K moves to the one below.

Use the Hide pinned toggle to reveal pinned tabs when needed.
